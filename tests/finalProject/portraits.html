Portraits

We offer individual neat, professional head shots, to natural and candid group photos. Through a in-studio session we are sure to capture the moments you wish to remember or gift to a loved one.

We provide framed prints, wall canvases, mini photo books, print boxes, coffee table albums and more. Our full ordering catalog is available for viewing in our studio for you to view during your session with us.

Unsure of how many prints you want to order? Because the cost of the photo session and the products are separate, feel free not to purchase products until after the photo session.
A typical studio family portrait package resembles the four-member Moon family:

  • A 1-hour private hour session in our studio and access to its props
  • A digital copy of your choice of 8 edited proofs
  • A 18"x24" framed portrait of the proof of your choice
  • 12 6"x4" printed photographs of up to 2 proofs of your choice
  • 24 2"x3" printed photographs of up to 2 proofs of your choice

This package begins typically at $350 but may vary; please request a consultation for a more accurate estimate.

To inquire about availability, special requests, and/or a detailed estimate, please request a consultation.
