Weddings

When it comes to weddings, new beginnings, there is nothing more important than having a skilled photographer to capture each precious moment to be later retold.

We know planning weddings can be stressful and pricey; but don't let finding a quality photographer be another source of stress. We offer several different packages at all different price points. With all of our packages, we guarantee quality photos without breaking the bank.

A typical wedding package resembles the Madison and Eli wedding:

  • Over 120 Guests
  • Custom "shot list" of 25+ edited proofs What's a "shot list"?
  • 20+ edited proofs of the ceremony
  • 15+ shots of the couple during the reception
  • 100+ shots of guests during the reception

This package begins typically at $2,700 but will vary depending on the number of locations, travel, and the types of poses and more. A more accurate estimate is available with a consultation.
Some venues we have experience working with include:

  • The Grand Ithaca Hotel
  • Cayuga Pier
  • The Finger Lakes Barn
  • The State Hotel

To inquire about availability, special requests, and/or a detailed estimate, please request a consultation.
