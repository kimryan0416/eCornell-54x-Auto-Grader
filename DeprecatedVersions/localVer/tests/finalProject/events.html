Events

As part of our on-location services, we offer to document any large group event you may have. Some large events we've documented, aside from weddings, are engagement parties, fundraiser galas, corporate dinners, and more.
Some venues we have experience working with include:

  • The Grand Ithaca Hotel
  • Cayuga Pier
  • The Finger Lakes Barn
  • The State Hotel

Pricing is based on the number of guests, the subject of the photos, and the duration of the event. Because these vary greatly depending on the type of event, we recommend booking a consultation appointment for an accurate estimate of costs.

A typical small event package resembles the 30th anniversary of Tommy and Ellie:

  • A guest list of less than 100
  • A 4 hour long ceremony and reception
  • Custom "shot list" of 25+ edited proofsWhat's a "shot list"?
  • Your choice of 15+ edited proofs
  • 50+ Shots covering the event and guests

This package begins typically at $900 but will vary depending on the number of locations, travel, and the types of poses and more. A more accurate estimate is available with a consultation.
