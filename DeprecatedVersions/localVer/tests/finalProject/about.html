About Us

Studio 54x was founded in 2012. Originally starting as a one-woman, private business, the studio has since expanded to owning a physical location with set backgrounds and props. Additionally, the studio now employs several photographers and editors to ensure only high quality work is produced.

The founder, Sarah Kim, has been taking photos since 1996, but has been doing so professionally since 2003. She has been located in Ithaca, NY from 2007 and has been servicing the Finger Lakes area since. As a long time Ithacan, she knows the area on the back of her hand, having built a list of outdoors locations she's scouted and hand-picked to be the most unique, beautiful backgrounds in the area.
