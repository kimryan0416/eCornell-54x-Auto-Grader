About Ithaca Harvest Festival

This year, the Tompkins Trust Company is presenting Ithaca's 35th Annual Apple Harvest Festival, otherwise known as "AppleFest."

This festival is centered around apples, and showcases the many local food companies that use fresh Ithaca produce. More than 100 vendors and over 35,000 people attend the festival.

To find other events like this one, check out The Downtown Ithaca Alliance, which hosts many popular events in Ithaca, NY.
