Cat Facts

Lifespan: 12-15 years
Habitat: Live near areas of human habitation
Diet: Carnivorous; rodents
Average Weight: 9 to 10 lbs, varies by breed
Conservation Status: Overpopulated

Cat Fun Facts

  • Cats have excellent night vision and can see at only one-sixth the light level required for human vision
  • Cats cannot survive on vegetarian/vegan diets
  • Cats place each hind paw (almost) directly in the print of the corresponding fore paw to minimize noise and visible tracks
  • Cats cannot taste sweetness and have less taste buds than humans
  • Their ears can rotate 180° to face forwards or be flattened back
  • To aid with navigation and sensation, cats have dozens of whiskers over their body, especially their faces
  • Most adult cats are lactose intolerant; the sugars in milk are not easily digested
  • Their eyes are farsighted (an adaptation for hunting), which means they can't focus on objects within 2 feet
  • Their noses are about thirty times more sensitive than humans
