Hotels

Ithaca has it all-- from luxury hotels to the affordable get aways-- and here are some of the best options available.

The Statler Hotel

  • ★★★★
  • $$$$$
  • Distance from the Festival: 25 Minute Walk/Bus Ride
  • Home of hotel major students at Cornell, The Statler Hotel is one of the most luxurious hotels that the greater Ithaca area has to offer. Although it is not quite near the festival venue, if you are looking to get a tour of the beautiful Ivy League campus, there is no better spot.
  • Book Now

Hilton Graden Inn

  • ★★★
  • $$$$
  • Located at the Festival Venue
  • Hilton Garden Inn is an affordable 3 star hoel located right next to Ithaca Apple Festival. Your go to option if you're looking to get your bang for your buck.
  • Book Now

Inns of Aurora

  • ★★★★
  • $$$
  • Distnace from the Festival: 40 Minute Drive
  • Aurora Inn, 25 miles away from Ithaca, is the perfect option for a family looking to soak in the scenic beauty of Ithaca as well as attending the Ithaca Apple Festival. Driving up in your own vehicle would be required as there is no other way to get to Ithaca from the Aurora Inn. With its Lakeside dining options as well as other activities, Aurora Inn can bring more variety to your get away than staying at any other places
  • Book Now

Ithaca Marriott Downtown

  • ★★★★
  • $$$
  • Located at the Festival Venue
  • Brand new to Ithaca downtown this year, Marriott Downtown has garnered positive reviews from all fronts. From a wide array of dining options in the Commons to affordable yet luxurious guest rooms, Marriott will undoubtedly be able to accomodate all festival-goers
  • Book Now
