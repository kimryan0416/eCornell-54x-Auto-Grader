Schedule

Hours of Operation: Fri. 12pm-6pm, Sat.-Sun. 10am-6pm

Friday's Activities: On the first day of the festival, there will be fun apple harvest activities for the children from 12-3pm. Starting at 3pm, there will be local bands performing every half hour.

Saturday and Sunday Activities: On Saturday and Sunday, throughout both days, there will be arts and crafts stations, free entertainment, food, handicapped access, and live music performances.
