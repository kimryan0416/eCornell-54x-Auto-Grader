The Downtown Ithaca Alliance is pleased to announce the 35th Annual Apple Harvest Festival Presented by Tompkins Trust Company. Last year was such a wonderful success, we are looking forward to working with you all again this year. Attracting an audience of over 35,000 people, the festival will be held on:

  • September 29 (Friday, 12 - 6pm)
  • September 30 (Saturday, 10am - 6pm)
  • October 1 (Sunday, 10am - 6pm)

Welcome to a colorful weekend, where food, art and culture are key words, and of course APPLES lots of APPLES!

Highlights...

  • Family Fun Zone - CRAFT Show
  • Live Music - Street Performances
  • Farmers Market - Food Vendors
  • ....AND LOTS OF APPLES!
