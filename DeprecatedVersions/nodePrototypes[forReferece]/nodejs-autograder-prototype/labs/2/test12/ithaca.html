Things To Do In Ithaca

Ithaca Farmer's Market

~1.1 miles to the farmer's market~

The farmer's market is an amazing place to visit not only to get your fresh vegetables, but incredible foods from around the world. Whether you want crepe's for breakfast, a fresh cup of coffe, or lunch from Nepal,this is the perfect place to enjoy yourself by the lake.

Robert H. Treman State Park

~4 miles to Robert H. Treman State Park~

This park is insanely fun on a hot day for a nice swim day! You're bound to see tons of other happy souls enjoying this park over the summer, but don't worry about it being too crowded -- the more the merrier!

Catherine Valley Winery

~21 miles to Catherine Valley Winery~

This winery has a beautiful view of the lake to enjoy while try some amazing bottles of wine. The inside of the winery is decorated beautifully to further enhance your wine tasting experience. Also, be sure to try the white wines, a specialty of upstate NY!

Watkins Glen State Park

~26 miles to Watkins Glen~

This state park calls for a great time on a nice sunny day. Be sure to bring a football to toss around and some chips and burgers to grill while you enjoy the great trails and waterfalls this park has to offer.

Corning Museum of Glass

~45 miles to Corning Museum of Glass~

This museum leads to a fun day for everyone in the family! You get to see everything from how glass is made to some of the most beautiful pieces you've ever seen. There are an array of colors in glass flowers and unreal decorations made to perfection.
