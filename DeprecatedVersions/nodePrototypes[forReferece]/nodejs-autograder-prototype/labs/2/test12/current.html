2017 Apple Fest

Friday September 29, 2017 ~ 12pm - 6pm

Saturday September 30, 2017 ~ 10am - 6pm

Sunday October 1, 2017 ~ 10am - 6pm

This year come celebrate the 35th Apple Festival, a local tradition in Ithaca, NY! With vendors filling up the Commons, you can find everything you could possible imagine in apple form from candy apples and cider to apple-infused savory meals. Come try tons of apple-inspried foods, buy some unique trinkets and goods from local vendors, and enjoy the entertainment. There's even a kid's corner indoors in the Commons!
