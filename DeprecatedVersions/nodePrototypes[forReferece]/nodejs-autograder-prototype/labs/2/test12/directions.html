Directions

By Car

NY Highways 13, 79, 89, and 96 all bring you to Ithaca.

If you are using a GPS, you can enter 171 E M.L.K. Jr. St, Ithaca, NY 14850 to get to the Commons.

By Bus

Popular bus options include Greyhound of Shortline. Trips stop in various cities, including NYC, so you can see which stop is closest to you!

Greyhound

The Greyhound bus has a NYC stop at Port Authority Bus Terminal and makes a stop at Ithaca Bus Terminal.

Phone: (607) 272-7930 or (800) 231-2222

Website: http://locations.greyhound.com/bus-stations/us/new-york/ithaca/bus-station-150898

Shortline

The Shortline bus also has a NYC stop at Port Authority Bus Terminal and stops at both Ithaca Bus Terminal and Port Authority Bus Terminal

Phone: (607) 277-8800 or (800) 631-8405

Website: https://web.coachusa.com/shortline/ss.tickets.asp
