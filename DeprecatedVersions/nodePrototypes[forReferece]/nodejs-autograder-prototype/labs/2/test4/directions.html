Directions

Directions from the Statler Hotel at Cornell University (10 minutes by car):

1) Turn right onto Campus Rd

2) Slight left onto Stewart Ave

3) Turn right onto East Seneca Street

Directions from Ithaca College (5 minutes by car):

1) Head North on Danby Road toward Morse Chain

2) Continue onto South Aurora Street

3) Turn left onto East Seneca Street
