Personal Experiences

Name: Alfred Wagner

"I have been a vendor at Ithaca's Apple Festival for about 10 years now. I love it. I meet all these local folk, and I think to myself, 'Wow, I have been blessed with such great people'. You'd think after living in Ithaca, NY, for 30 years now, I'd get bored of it, but I haven't. I have thrived here doing what I love. Now, that you've heard my story, are you going to buy one of these?"

More photos found on Andrew Halpern's flikr.

Name: Cedric Castillo

"This is my first time at the Apple Festival, and I have got to say it is definitely exceeding my expectations. I come from a place in NY that isn't really well known because we're not the "city", but quite honestly, can you find something like this in the city? Maybe. It won't be the same though, that's for sure."

Taken by Kelly Fam.

Name: Christine Yang

"Yes, I am holding two containers of apple cider. Why? Why not? I have to get my fill of apple cider before the festival is over. Now, these are like my babies, so no, you cannot have any."

Taken by Kelly Fam.

Name: Helena Kim

"It is also my first time at the Apple Festival, and boy, does this fried dough taste so good. I am an absolute sucker for food like this. If you're afraid this festival is too apple-ly for you, there's so much more to this festival than the apples. Local resturants (not affiliated with apples) are also here. Ooo, I see my favorite, Taste of Thai. Gotta go."

Taken by Kelly Fam.
