Ithaca, NY

Ithaca, NY is located in upstate NY, a city in the Southern Tier Finger Lakes region of New York. Ithaca lies in Tompkins county, and is the largest community in this county. Home to two well-known colleges Ithaca College and Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, is filled with a diverse group of people, varying from college students to families, professors, you name it. Ithaca is home to gorges and natural beauties within a 10 mile radius. [2]

Ithaca's Apple Festival will be held in Ithaca Commons, a pedestrian shopping area that will be filled with vendors from all over upstate New York. Enjoy the festival in the heart of downtown Ithaca. [3]
