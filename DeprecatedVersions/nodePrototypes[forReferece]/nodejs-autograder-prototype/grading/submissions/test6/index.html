As someone who was raised in New York City, I had always thought of anything beyond Westchester as a midde-of-nowhere, rural upstate that doesn't have much to offer for me. Boy I was wrong.

There is a lot more to New York State outside of our cities. And every year, the town of Ithaca hosts the Apple Harvest Festival; this year from September 30th to October 2nd. After years of helping my friends and families plan out their trip out to Ithaca for an amazing weekend, I've come to realize that my resources can come in handy for many New Yorkeres. From expected vendors, events, to accomodations, this website should come in handy when planning your trip up to Ithaca from the city. Enjoy!
