Food and Beverages

Ithaca Apple Festival each year showcases some of Ithaca's best local resturants, organic farms, breweries, wineries, and more. If it weren't for the food, I'm not sure the festival would be as popular as it is. Most vendors accept cards, but just in case be sure to bring cash.
