About

Ithaca is no stranger to agricultural festivals and this year is no different. Returning for it's 35th year, Ithaca is celebrating the start of fall with its annual Apple Harvest Festival. Once again, residents, students, and tourists, alike will be able to sample local food, enjoy performances, and browse merchandise.

Throughout the Ithaca-Tompkins county region there is tremendous diversity in agriculture. Anna Streikhaus of Littletree Orchards realzied this in 1982 and wanted a way to showcase locally-grown foods in an effort to garner a better respect and appreciation for the bounty of agriculture. And so she started the Apple Harvest Festival. Littletree Orchards continues this commitment yearly by bringing back their renowned apple cider donuts.

From local farm stands to children's activties to handmade accessories, there's something for everyone at Apple Fest! Different performances by student groups from the local colleges will create a youthful environment while local groups will showcase the talent of Ithaca.

All this said, Ithaca's 35th Annual Apple Harvest Festival is a "can't miss"!
